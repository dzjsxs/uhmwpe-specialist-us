Product Introduction

Ultra High Molecular Weight Polyethylene (UHMWPE) Sheet is one of the most advanced engineering plastics available today. With a molecular weight ranging from 3.5 to 9.5 million g/mol, UHMWPE exhibits a unique combination of properties that make it superior to standard polyethylene grades. It is produced through compression molding or ram extrusion processes, resulting in a dense, homogeneous structure with exceptional wear resistance and impact strength.

UHMWPE sheet is widely recognized as the material of choice in demanding industrial applications where conventional materials fail. Its self-lubricating surface, combined with outstanding chemical resistance, makes it ideal for environments involving abrasive materials, corrosive chemicals, and extreme mechanical stress. Jiasheng Plastic manufactures UHMWPE sheets with molecular weight ≥5 million g/mol, ensuring premium performance across all application scenarios.
