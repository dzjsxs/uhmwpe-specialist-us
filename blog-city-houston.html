Houston, Texas, is the undisputed energy capital of the world. Home to more than 4,600 energy-related firms and the headquarters of 24 of the world's largest oil and gas companies, the Greater Houston area drives an extraordinary demand for industrial materials that can withstand the extreme pressures, temperatures, and chemical exposures of hydrocarbon processing. Engineering plastics — particularly UHMWPE, HDPE, PTFE, and nylon — have become indispensable in Houston's refineries, petrochemical plants, offshore support facilities, and pipeline infrastructure.

UHMWPE and HDPE for Petrochemical Applications

Houston's petrochemical corridor along the Ship Channel and in the Baytown, Pasadena, and La Porte areas operates some of the world's largest chemical processing facilities. HDPE sheet and rod are widely used for chemical storage tank liners, pump housings, and pipe fittings due to their excellent resistance to acids, alkalis, and hydrocarbons. UHMWPE is preferred for applications requiring both chemical resistance and extreme wear resistance, such as slurry pump liners, impeller wear rings, and chemical conveyor components. Our HDPE and UHMWPE products are available in natural, black, and antistatic grades, with FDA-compliant options for food-grade chemical processing.

Ground Protection Mats for Oilfield and Pipeline Construction

The Texas oilfield — from the Permian Basin to the Eagle Ford Shale and the Gulf Coast — requires constant construction and maintenance activity on terrain ranging from soft clay soils to environmentally sensitive wetlands. Our UHMWPE ground protection mats are the preferred solution for creating temporary access roads and working platforms for drilling rigs, workover units, pipeline construction crews, and heavy crane operations. Available in standard 1200mm × 2400mm × 20mm size and custom dimensions up to 3000mm × 6000mm, our mats can support loads exceeding 80 tonnes per mat and interlock to create continuous roadways of any length.

PTFE and Nylon Components for Refinery Applications

Houston's 12 major oil refineries — including the ExxonMobil Baytown complex, the largest in the US — rely on PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) for gaskets, seals, valve seats, and pipe linings in high-temperature, high-pressure service. Our PTFE sheet, rod, and tube products are manufactured to ASTM D1457 specifications and are available in virgin, glass-filled, carbon-filled, and bronze-filled grades. Nylon 6 and Nylon 66 components are widely used in Houston refineries for gear blanks, wear pads, slide bearings, and structural components where metal replacement reduces weight and eliminates corrosion.
