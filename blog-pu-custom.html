Beyond standard sheets and rods, Jiasheng Plastic offers full custom fabrication of polyurethane components — from CNC-machined bushings and rollers to molded screen panels with metal inserts. Our cast PU process allows precise control of hardness, color, and dimensions for OEM and replacement part applications.

Machining Polyurethane

Cast polyurethane can be machined on standard CNC lathes and mills. Key parameters: sharp carbide tools with positive rake angles, lower cutting speeds than rigid plastics, water-based coolant to prevent heat buildup. Tolerances of ±0.1mm achievable for most dimensions.

Molded Components with Metal Inserts

Metal inserts can be incorporated during the casting process for components requiring threaded fasteners or metal reinforcement. The insert is placed in the mold before pouring, and the liquid PU bonds to the metal surface. This produces composite components with PU wear resistance and steel structural strength.

Our Custom Fabrication Capabilities

Cut-to-size sheets and strips from standard cast blanks; CNC-machined bushings, rollers, and wear rings from rod stock; open-cast molding for simple shapes; closed-mold casting for complex shapes; metal insert molding; bonding to steel and aluminum substrates; custom colors and hardness grades.

OEM and Replacement Parts

We supply OEM polyurethane components to equipment manufacturers and replacement parts to end users. Typical lead times: 10–20 business days for custom machined parts, 20–30 days for new mold tooling. Minimum order quantities are flexible for prototype and small-batch orders.
