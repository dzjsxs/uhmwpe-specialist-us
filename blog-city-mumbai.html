Mumbai, India's financial capital and largest port city, serves as the commercial gateway to one of the world's fastest-growing industrial economies. The Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR) and the broader Maharashtra industrial belt host major clusters in petrochemicals (Reliance Industries' Jamnagar complex, HPCL, BPCL), pharmaceuticals (India's largest pharma hub), textiles, food processing, and a rapidly expanding infrastructure and construction sector. India's ambitious National Infrastructure Pipeline (NIP) — targeting USD 1.4 trillion in infrastructure investment — is driving unprecedented demand for engineering materials including high-performance plastics.

UHMWPE and HDPE for Indian Petrochemical Industry

India's petrochemical sector — centred on Reliance Industries' Jamnagar refinery complex (the world's largest), ONGC's operations, and the Dahej and Hazira chemical clusters in Gujarat — requires extensive UHMWPE and HDPE components for chemical storage, pipeline protection, and process equipment. Our HDPE pipe-grade materials meet IS 4984 (Indian Standard equivalent to ISO 4427) and are available in PE80 and PE100 grades. For chemical plant applications, we supply PTFE-lined HDPE pipe, PVDF sheet, and custom-fabricated chemical storage tanks.

Nylon and POM for Indian Automotive and Textile Machinery

India's automotive manufacturing sector — with Pune, Chennai, and Gurugram as major production hubs — and the country's world-leading textile machinery industry centred in Ahmedabad, Surat, and Coimbatore, create strong demand for precision engineering plastic components. Our PA6 (Nylon 6) is manufactured to IS 11785 specifications and is widely used for gear blanks, bushings, and structural components in textile looms, spinning machines, and automotive assemblies. We supply PA6 rods, sheets, and tubes in standard and custom sizes, with oil-filled grades for self-lubricating bearing applications.

Ground Protection Mats for Indian Infrastructure Projects

India's massive infrastructure expansion — including the Mumbai-Ahmedabad High Speed Rail (Bullet Train project), the Mumbai Coastal Road, the Navi Mumbai International Airport, and the Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor — creates enormous demand for temporary ground protection solutions. Our UHMWPE ground protection mats are specified for construction sites with soft, waterlogged, or unstable ground conditions, providing stable working platforms for tower cranes, concrete pumps, and heavy plant. The mats' UV-stabilised formulation is designed for India's intense solar radiation conditions.

Shipping to Mumbai: JNPT Port

We ship to Mumbai via Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust (JNPT) — India's largest container port, handling over 5 million TEUs annually — located at Nhava Sheva, approximately 55km from central Mumbai. Standard sea freight transit time from our factory in Hebei Province, China, to JNPT is 12-16 days. We also ship to Mundra Port (Gujarat) for customers in western India, with similar transit times. Import duties on engineering plastics to India are typically 7.5-10% BCD plus GST, with preferential rates available under the India-ASEAN FTA for certain product categories.

Indian Standards (BIS) and Certifications

Our products for the Indian market comply with relevant BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) standards including IS 4984 (HDPE pipes), IS 11785 (Nylon 6), and IS 7328 (HDPE for general purposes). For pharmaceutical and food applications, products comply with IS 10146 (plastics in contact with food) and FDA 21 CFR requirements. Third-party test certificates from SGS India, Bureau Veritas India, or Intertek India are available upon request.
