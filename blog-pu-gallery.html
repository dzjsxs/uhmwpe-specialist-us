A comprehensive overview of our polyurethane product range — standard cast PU sheets in yellow, green, red, and black; round rod stock for machining; custom molded parts with metal inserts; screen panels; conveyor components; and finished wear parts ready for export from Dezhou, Shandong, China.

Cast Polyurethane Sheets

We manufacture cast polyurethane sheets in hardness grades from Shore A 20 to Shore D 60. Standard colors: yellow (Shore A 80), green (Shore A 60), red (Shore A 90), black (UV-resistant, Shore A 70–85). Standard sheet sizes: 1000×2000mm, 1200×2400mm, 1500×3000mm. Thickness: 5–200mm. Custom sizes available with 10–15 day lead time.

Polyurethane Rods and Tubes

Round rod stock from 10mm to 300mm diameter, lengths to 2000mm. Available in Shore A 60–95 and Shore D 40–60. Used for machining bushings, rollers, wear rings, and custom components. Tubes available for hollow shaft applications.

Custom Molded Components

We produce custom molded polyurethane components including: screen panels with bolt-down inserts, pump impellers and liners, hydrocyclone components, dock bumpers, roller covers, and custom wear parts. Minimum order quantities flexible for prototype orders.

Export Packaging and Delivery

All products are packaged for safe international shipping: sheets wrapped in PE film and packed in wooden crates, rods bundled and wrapped, custom parts individually protected. Standard export terms: FOB Qingdao or Tianjin. Lead time: 10–20 business days for standard products, 20–30 days for custom orders.
