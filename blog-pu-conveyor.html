Polyurethane Components for Conveyor Systems

Impact Bars, Skirt Boards, Wear Strips, and Roller Covers in Cast PU

Jiasheng Plastic | Dezhou, Shandong, China | Cast Polyurethane Manufacturer
Home › Blog › Polyurethane Components for Conveyor Systems...

Conveyor systems in mining, bulk handling, and manufacturing rely on polyurethane for impact absorption, wear protection, and noise reduction. Cast PU components offer the ideal combination of properties for demanding conveyor applications.

Impact Bars

At conveyor transfer points, PU impact bars (Shore A 50–65) absorb the energy of falling material while protecting the belt. Typical dimensions: 150×100mm cross-section, lengths to 2000mm. Attachment via T-slot or bolt-down to steel support frame. Service life 2–4× longer than rubber impact bars in abrasive applications.

Skirt Board Liners

PU skirt board liners (Shore A 60–75) provide a flexible seal that conforms to belt irregularities while resisting abrasion from conveyed material. Available in standard widths (50–200mm) and lengths to 2000mm. Significantly longer life than rubber skirt boards.

Wear Strips and Guide Rails

PU wear strips protect conveyor structure from abrasion and guide the belt along its path. Available in standard profiles (flat, L-shaped, T-shaped) or custom cross-sections. Hardness Shore A 70–85 for wear strip applications. Can be bonded or mechanically fastened.

Roller Covers

PU roller covers provide grip and protect conveyor rollers from abrasion. Available in Shore A 60–80, with smooth or grooved surface profiles. Particularly effective in wet applications where rubber covers would deteriorate. Can be applied to new or refurbished rollers.
