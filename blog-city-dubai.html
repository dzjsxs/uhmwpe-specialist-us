Dubai and the wider UAE stand as the Middle East's most dynamic construction and energy market. With over AED 300 billion in active construction projects, a world-class port infrastructure at Jebel Ali, and a thriving oil & gas sector extending from Abu Dhabi's ADNOC operations to offshore platforms in the Arabian Gulf, the Emirates generate enormous demand for high-performance engineering plastics. From temporary ground protection on mega-project construction sites to UHMWPE marine fender pads at Dubai's expanding port facilities, our products serve the full spectrum of UAE industrial applications.

UHMWPE Marine Fender Pads for Dubai Ports

Dubai's maritime infrastructure — including Jebel Ali Port (the world's largest man-made harbour), Port Rashid, and the expanding Dubai Maritime City — requires continuous supply of UHMWPE fender pads, dock bumpers, and berthing systems. UHMWPE's exceptional impact resistance, low coefficient of friction, and resistance to seawater make it the material of choice for marine applications. Our UHMWPE fender pads are available in standard and custom profiles, with thicknesses from 20mm to 200mm and surface finishes optimised for low-friction vessel contact.

Ground Protection Mats for UAE Mega-Projects

Dubai's construction boom — from Expo City Dubai's legacy projects to Palm Jebel Ali, Dubai Creek Harbour, and the ongoing expansion of Al Maktoum International Airport — creates constant demand for temporary ground protection solutions. Our UHMWPE ground protection mats provide stable working platforms for tower cranes, concrete pumps, and heavy plant on the sandy, soft, or waterlogged ground conditions common to Dubai's coastal development sites. The mats' UV-stabilised formulation ensures they maintain structural integrity under the intense UAE solar radiation.

HDPE Systems for UAE Infrastructure

The UAE's rapid infrastructure development — including the expansion of the Dubai Metro, new highway interchanges, and the Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum Solar Park — requires extensive HDPE drainage, ducting, and containment systems. Our HDPE sheet and pipe products are manufactured to ISO 4427 and EN 12201 standards and are available in SDR 11, SDR 17, and SDR 26 pressure ratings. For Abu Dhabi's ADNOC and its operating companies, we supply HDPE and UHMWPE components for chemical storage, pipeline protection, and process equipment.
